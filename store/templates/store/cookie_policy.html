Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Fishy Friend Aquatics ("we," "us," "our," or the "Company") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website and services. By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet, etc.) when you visit a website. They store information about your browsing activity and preferences to enhance your user experience. There are two main types of cookies:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them

2. TYPES OF COOKIES WE USE

2.1 Essential/Necessary Cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function properly and provide essential services:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
sessionid Maintains your user session and authentication Session/30 days
csrftoken Security token to prevent cross-site request forgery attacks Session
cart_items Stores items in your shopping cart 30 days
user_preferences Saves your website preferences and settings 1 year

Note: These cookies cannot be disabled as they are essential for website functionality. Blocking them may prevent proper operation of our services.

2.2 Functional/Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your choices and settings to provide a personalized experience:

  • Language and region preferences
  • Display preferences (light/dark mode)
  • Saved items and wishlist
  • Previously viewed products

Consent Required: You can control these cookies through your browser or account settings.

2.3 Performance/Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how you use our website, allowing us to improve site performance and user experience:

  • Pages you visit and time spent on each page
  • Links you click and actions you take
  • Website navigation patterns
  • Error tracking and performance monitoring
  • Traffic sources and device information

We use analytics tools such as Google Analytics for this purpose. Your data is anonymized and aggregated.

2.4 Marketing/Advertising Cookies

These cookies track your interests and behavior to deliver personalized marketing content and advertisements:

  • Retargeting ads based on visited products
  • Personalized product recommendations
  • Campaign performance tracking
  • Social media integration

Note: These cookies may be set by third-party advertising partners.

5. YOUR COOKIE CHOICES AND CONTROLS

5.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers provide options to control cookies. You can typically:

  • Accept or reject all cookies
  • Accept only essential cookies
  • Choose which cookies to accept from which websites
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set privacy levels for cookie acceptance

Instructions for common browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Clear browsing data

5.2 Cookie Consent Management

When you first visit our website, you will be presented with a cookie consent notice allowing you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Accept only essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences
  • Access this cookie policy

You can update your preferences at any time in your account settings or by clicking the cookie banner.

5.3 Opt-Out Options

To opt-out of specific types of cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Behavioral Advertising: Visit the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool
  • Marketing Emails: Click "Unsubscribe" in any marketing email

6. DO NOT TRACK (DNT)

Some browsers include Do Not Track features. Currently, there is no industry standard for recognizing DNT signals. Our website does not respond to DNT browser signals, but you can use browser settings to control cookies.

7. CONSEQUENCES OF BLOCKING COOKIES

If you choose to block or disable cookies, certain features and functionality of our website may not work properly, including:

  • User authentication and account access
  • Shopping cart functionality
  • Order processing and checkout
  • Personalization features
  • Website performance optimization

We recommend allowing at least essential cookies to ensure a complete browsing experience.

8. DATA SECURITY

Information collected through cookies is protected by encryption and secure server infrastructure. However, no data transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of cookie data.

9. CHANGES TO COOKIES

As technology evolves, we may add new cookies or modify existing ones. Updated information will be reflected in this Cookie Policy. Significant changes will be communicated through email notifications.

10. INTERNATIONAL USERS
