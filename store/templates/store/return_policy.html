Return Policy

We want you to be happy with every aquatic purchase. Review the details below to understand our return process.

1. Eligibility

Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery for unopened dry goods. Live stock is evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

2. Condition of Items

Items must be unused, in the original packaging, and accompanied by proof of purchase.

3. Refund Process

Approved returns are refunded to the original payment method once the item is inspected.

4. Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item arrived damaged or incorrect.

Contact the Help Center to initiate a return or clarify any questions.
